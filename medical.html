Problem Objective

The Medical Website has to be web-accessible and compliant while having a creative user interface. Regardless of age or sight impairment, users need to be able to use the app/website. Patients struggle to find some of the important functions on the website. Too much unnecessary information may distract them, the shift is too small, or the website is too dark.

The Result

The medical website is patient/consumer-oriented, which allows visitors to easily find the treatment information they are looking for. The website features simple navigation to pages and to essential functions.

The website's content addressed consumers' desire to find services relating to their issue while also focusing on treatment choices and care particular to 312 Medical Clinic.
